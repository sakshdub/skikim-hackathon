Trekking in Sikkim

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the pristine Himalayan wilderness.

Popular Trekking Routes

Goecha La Trek

Get up close with Mt. Kanchenjunga.

One of the most spectacular treks, offering grand views of the Kanchenjunga peak from the Goecha La pass.

Duration: 9-11 Days Difficulty: Difficult

Dzongri Trek

A shorter trek with stunning mountain views.

Ideal for those short on time, this trek takes you to the Dzongri viewpoint for a magnificent sunrise over the Kanchenjunga range.

Duration: 5-7 Days Difficulty: Moderate

Kasturi Orar Trek

A circular trek through remote valleys.

This challenging circular trek covers the Goecha La pass and explores the remote landscapes of the Kasturi Orar valley.

Duration: 12-15 Days Difficulty: Difficult

Green Lake Trek

Adventure to the base of Kanchenjunga.

A strenuous and rewarding trek to the pristine Green Lake, the base camp of Mount Kanchenjunga, surrounded by towering peaks.

Duration: 13-16 Days Difficulty: Strenuous
