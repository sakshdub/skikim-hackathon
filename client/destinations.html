Sikkim's Destinations

Discover the iconic towns, sacred sites, and natural wonders of the Himalayas.

Gangtok

The vibrant capital, offering a blend of modernity and tradition with stunning views.

Pelling

Famous for its breathtaking views, monasteries, and the glass Skywalk.

Namchi

The cultural capital, home to giant statues of Char Dham and Samdruptse Hill.

Yumthang Valley

The "Valley of Flowers," a paradise of alpine meadows and hot springs in North Sikkim.

Nathula Pass

A high-altitude pass on the Indo-China border, offering a unique and thrilling experience.

Gurudongmar Lake

One of the highest lakes in the world, revered for its sacred waters and stunning beauty.

Baba Harbhajan Singh Temple

A unique shrine dedicated to a soldier of the Indian army, believed to protect the region.

Samdruptse Hill

Home to the world's largest statue of Guru Padmasambhava, offering panoramic views.

Khecheopalri Lake

A sacred "wishing lake" for both Buddhists and Hindus, nestled in a pristine forest.

Khangchendzonga National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage site, home to the snow leopard, red panda, and stunning Himalayan landscapes.

Maenam Wildlife Sanctuary

Located above Ravangla, it offers rich biodiversity and a popular trek to Maenam Peak for panoramic views.

Fambong Lho Wildlife Sanctuary

A large sanctuary near Gangtok, protecting a dense forest of oak, katus, and various species of rhododendron.
